Key Metrics Explained

  • New clients we interacted with: Total number of new clients engaged during the period
  • Clients signed engagement letter: Clients who have formally engaged with our services through signed agreements
  • Clients we helped got housing: Individuals successfully housed with UEO support
  • LifeLine Phones give away: Phones distributed through the LifeLine program to clients in need
  • Housing applications: Total number of housing applications submitted for clients
  • Active Volunteers (non students): Unique volunteers present in all 3 months of the quarter (non-student volunteers)
  • Volunteer hours: Total volunteer hours contributed during the period
  • Active Clients: New clients who are actively engaged with UEO services, meaning they have both signed an engagement letter and have an “Active” status
  • Semi-Active Clients: New clients who are somewhat engaged with UEO services, meaning they have signed an engagement letter and have a “Semi Active” status, indicating they may need additional support to become fully active
